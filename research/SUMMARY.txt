KEY FINDINGS:
-------------
1. COMMIT-REVEAL is the most practical immediate solution for COVENANT:
   - Adds ~20k gas per bid (acceptable for high-value tasks)
   - Requires 2 transactions (commit + reveal)
   - Completely prevents front-running and bid sniping

2. VICKREY AUCTIONS provide incentive compatibility:
   - Winner pays second-highest price
   - Bidders bid true valuations (no strategic shading)
   - Best for auctioning high-value tasks

3. TWAP ORACLE prevents manipulation:
   - Rejects tasks with prices >3x historical average
   - Uses weighted arithmetic mean over 7-day window
   - Integrates with TaskMarket.postTask()

4. FLASHBOTS PROTECT is zero-cost on-chain:
   - Frontend/SDK routes transactions to private RPC
   - No contract changes required
   - Can add "flashbotsPreferred" flag to tasks

5. FHE is forward-compatible (2025):
   - Zama fhEVM allows encrypted bid comparisons
   - ~5-10x gas overhead (use for >$1k tasks only)
   - TaskAuctionFHE.sol is ready for testnet deployment

SECURITY CONSIDERATIONS:
------------------------
- Commit-reveal requires non-zero ETH deposits to prevent spam
- Vickrey settlement: use off-chain computation + Merkle proof for gas efficiency
- TWAP: use geometric mean to resist single-block manipulation
- Flashbots: decentralize builder whitelist via EigenLayer
- FHE: design fallback to commit-reveal if decryption times out
